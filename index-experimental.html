How the Rotation Planner works

Plan a Persona 5: The Phantom X boss rotation. Build your team, script each turn, then share it as a link or image, or run it live while you fight. Everything you type is saved automatically in your browser.

Toolbar

  • Guide: this help.
  • Load Examples: open a finished MLD or DOD rotation to see how a plan looks (replaces your current work).
  • Import: bring a rotation in from a JSON file, a share code or link, or plain text pasted from Discord. A text import fills in what it recognizes and highlights in yellow whatever still needs your attention.
  • Export: save or share your work as a JSON backup, a PNG image (with art, without art, or turns only), a Discord-ready Text version, a Share Link (editable or read-only view), or a short Code.
  • Settings: per-device look and feel (theme, which note fields show, which side the persona art sits on, the default layout). Never changes your rotation or exports.
  • Reset: start over with a blank plan.

Setup

Fill in the header (Name, Mode, Boss, Patch, Expected Score, Credits) so your rotation is properly labelled. Everything is required for export except the Expected Score.

Team

Four slots plus the Elucidator. Pick each character (type to filter the list; in-game names and nicknames work too), then set Awareness, Reforge and the Revelation cards. The usual card set is filled in for you, and you can still change it. Each card also has its own Notes line.

Special fields appear by themselves where they matter: Wonder's dagger, the Twins' role (with its dual-element symbols), and Fuuka's backup character (which gets its own build card). Drag a card by its grip or its image to reorder the slots, and use Whale to max everyone to A6/R6 at once.

Below the team, add up to three Personas (the signature skill fills in automatically, plus two teachable skills) and a Team Notes box for things like the required Pierce or Crit.

Rotation

Each turn has a name, an optional note and a list of actions. Press Auto Fill first to drop your team into every turn, so you only have to type the actions (Clear empties them again). For each action, pick the character and tap a button (S1 to S3, HL/Theurgy, Attack/Gun/Guard); a few characters have an extra signature button such as Violet's “Masquerade”. Wonder also picks a persona and skill. A free text line is always there for the target or a note.

If an action still points at a character or persona you've swapped out, or the characters in a turn don't act in your team order, the turn turns red and Export is blocked, so you never share a broken plan. Auto Correct offers one-click fixes (with undo) for most of it. Reorder turns by dragging their header, and duplicate or delete them. In DOD the last two turns are breaks, pinned at the end.

Play Mode

Press Play to run the rotation live while you fight. It is a clean full-screen view that shows one step at a time with big character faces, so you can follow your plan at a glance from a phone or a second screen.

  • Turn or Action: show a whole turn at once, or focus a single action with a “what's next” preview.
  • Vertical or Horizontal: choose the layout that fits your screen (it adapts on its own on phones in landscape).
  • In Turn mode, tap each action to check it off as you go; checking the last one loads the next turn. Swipe or use Prev/Next to move through the fight, and the reset and restart buttons jump back to the start.

Live Preview

The Rotation Preview toggle below the turns shows exactly how the image export will look and updates as you type. Use the Zoom slider (or pinch and ctrl-scroll right inside it) to inspect the details up close. It's never part of the exported file.

Saving & sharing

Your work autosaves in this browser. For a backup, use the JSON export; for a picture to post, use a PNG; to send it to a teammate, use a Share Link or Copy Code. The whole rotation is packed into the link itself, so nothing is uploaded to a server. Share links shorten automatically and also hand you a ready-made, Discord-friendly masked link.
